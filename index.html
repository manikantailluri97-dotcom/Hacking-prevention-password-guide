Common Cyber Threats

🎣 Phishing

Fraudulent emails or websites that trick you into giving up sensitive information.

💻 Malware

Malicious software designed to damage, disrupt, or gain unauthorised access to your system.

🔓 Brute Force

Automated guessing of passwords until the correct one is found.

👤 Social Engineering

Manipulating people psychologically to reveal confidential information.

🕵️ Spyware

Software that secretly monitors your activity and sends data to third parties.

💰 Ransomware

Encrypts your files and demands payment to restore access.

Security Dashboard

You're logged in! Here are essential cybersecurity tips to stay safe online.

01

Never Share Passwords

Your password is personal. No legitimate service will ever ask for it via email or chat.

02

Beware of Phishing

Check sender email addresses carefully. Phishing emails mimic real brands to steal your info.

03

Use Strong Passwords

A good password has 12+ characters, mixed case, numbers, and special symbols.

04

Enable Two-Factor Auth

2FA adds an extra verification step, making it much harder for attackers to access your account.

05

Update Software Regularly

Security patches fix known vulnerabilities. Always keep your OS and apps up to date.

06

Use Secure Networks

Avoid logging into sensitive accounts over public Wi-Fi. Use a VPN when necessary.
